CloudSync AI - Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
You can create an account by clicking the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and entering your email and password.

How do I reset my password?
Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password". Follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I change my email address?
Navigate to Account Settings and update your email under the profile section.

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes, subscriptions can be cancelled at any time from the billing section of your dashboard.

Do you offer refunds?
Refunds are available within 14 days of purchase for monthly plans. Annual plans are non-refundable after activation.

Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 7-day free trial for all new users.

How do I contact support?
You can contact support via the chat widget or email support@cloudsyncai.com.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your subscription plan at any time from your account dashboard.

Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect user data.

Do you support team collaboration?
Yes, team features are available in the Pro and Enterprise plans.
